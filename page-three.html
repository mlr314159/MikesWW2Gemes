Figures

Showing how Figures & Modles are painted and fixed on to bases.

********

Figures are 10mm Pendraken miniatures
Bases are 40mmx40mm for Infantry, Anti-Tank and Artillary.
Bases are 40mmx60mm for Tanks.

The initial games, to be played, will use the scenarios from "One Hour Wargames"
And the period will be late 1941 in Egypt. The armies will be 8th Army vs Afrika Corps.
For each army the maximum number of units for any game is:-
4 x Motorised Infantry, 2 x Tanks, 2 x Artillary, 2 x Anti-Tank;

So models needed per army are:
Motorised Infantry, 4 * 14 = 56 infantry figures + 4 transport:
Tanks, 2 * 2 tanks = 4 tanks (8th army - Crusader II tanks, AK - Panzer III short 5cm)
Artillary, 2 * 1 gun and crew, 1 tractor = 2 guns + 2 tractor (8th army - 25pdr, AK - 10.5cm)
Anti-Tank, 2 * 1 gun and crew, 1 tractor = 2 guns + 2 tractor (8th army - 2pdr, AK - 5cm)

the models are made with a cast metal alloy, they need to be washed and dried. They are then undercoated in thin white primer, pained to a pleaseing level of datail, then glued to thier base. Some modelling can be done to make the based models look more artistic.
