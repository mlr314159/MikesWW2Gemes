Playing toy soldiers theamed on WW2

About Rules Figures Tables Games

Rules

Rules to play battles with toy soldiers based upon WW2.

Things Needed to play the game, outside of rules and figures.

Stuff needed

  • Based and painted figures and models from Pendraken 10mm
  • Handfull of Normal Dice - refered to as D6 - 1D6 or a D6 means 1 dice, 2D6 means 2 dice
  • Handfull of small red Normal Dice used to record casulalties
  • Ruller measured in inches, a normal 12inch ruller is fine.
  • Table 4 x4 foot, usually covered in suitablly cooloured cloth

Units used in the Game

Most Units in the game start out with 2 bases that remain next to each other. Some special units can start the game with just one unit.

Infanrty Units - (fight on foot)

Infantry

Infanrty Unit

Unit of Standard foot soldiers : 2 Bases of 8 figures

Moterised Infantry

Moterised Infanrty Unit

Unit of Standard foot soldiers : 2 Bases of : 1st base 6 figures and Transport : 2nd base 8 figures
