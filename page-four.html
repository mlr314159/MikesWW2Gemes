Games

Report of battles played with the rules and figuers using various scenarios.

The initial games, to be played, will use the scenarios from "One Hour Wargames"
And the period will be late 1941 in Egypt. The armies will be 8th Army vs Afrika Corps.
My intention is to play the 30 scenarios in order, but have a fixed list of units that can be used over the whole series of games.
Any unit destroyed in a game is reomed from the rosta of units available in next game.
Any unit where only one base survives a game goes into reinforcements pool and can be reinforced over time, and when back to full strength goes back into rosta of units available in next game.
Any unit where both bases survive a game remains in rosta of units available in next game, back to full strength.

Initial forces in both armies are :- 55 * Motorised Infantry, 15 * Tank, 15 * Artillary, 15 * Anti-Tank
